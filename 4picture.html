Your chess questions, answered

How do I join the community?

Click the sign-up button and fill in your details to become part of our chess family. Once you're in, you can play, track your progress, and meet fellow enthusiasts.

Can I play against an AI?

Definitely! Select ‘Play vs Computer’ to challenge an AI at any level. It’s a friendly way to practice, learn, and grow at your own pace.

How do I review my games?

Your dashboard keeps a record of every game you play. Dive in to revisit your moves, analyze strategies, and download games for deeper study.

What training tools are available?

Head to the training room for interactive lessons, puzzles, and practice matches. Whether you’re starting out or aiming higher, there’s something for everyone.
